FireFume - An Interactive Wildfire Spread Detection Model

With FireFume you are able to predict next day wildfire growth. Interactive Wildfire Application leveraging deep learning techniques. Uses Tensorflow and Keras. Libraries like matplotlib and numpy. Our team utilizes cutting edge deep learning architecture such as Convolutional Autoencoders and Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)


Why you may want to use our AI app:

  • High Accuracy Fire Predictions
  • Colorful Image Displays
  • New Dataset using Remote-Sensing Data and Satellite Imaging
